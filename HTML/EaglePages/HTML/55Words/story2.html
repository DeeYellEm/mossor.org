An Affair to Remember

by Bill Wickart 
"Dear John,"
Success in 8 months. Drove Mary to my best friend. No alimony, minimal child support, bliss with lovely Anika.

"We have the children.  We are in love, and will raise them well.
Will write later when settled."
I punched Anika's number. On the third ring, I noticed the signatures:

	    "Sincerely,
		Mary and Anika"
