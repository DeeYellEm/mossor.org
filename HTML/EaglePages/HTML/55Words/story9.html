The Mystery of the Mislabelled Coffee Pot

by Hal Reichardt
Orange top again, he thought as he spun the decaf top off the pot of regular coffee with a disdainful twist.

Later that night, our intrepid hero jumped from his hiding place and confronted the man he had always suspected.

"What does orange mean, mister?"

The janitor looked a bit puzzled.

"Is good fruit, no?"
