Passages

by Erin Fassio
Why wouldn't he shut up? She was tired of listening to his talk about work as a programmer or his softball team. Everything in her body language spoke of her disinterest; still he droned on. Tomorrow she would leave him, 10 years wasted. She had better things to do when she had no clothes on.
