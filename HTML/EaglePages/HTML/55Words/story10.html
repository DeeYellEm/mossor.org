Pure Love

by Mick Posner
Mandy ran her hands down the strong curved body, her thoughts were of pure ecstasy. What secrets did it hold, would it perform, could it perform, it's strange color and shape intrigued her. The only thing left to do was to purchase this new kayak and try it out on some real white water.
