Status Quo

by Erin Fassio
The first light awoke ancestral memories eons old. She dug deep into the mud, deeper than she had ever dug before. She covered her eyes with mud and made sure her nose was covered in a pourous mixture of mud and rocks.

As Asia, America, Europe, and Africa assured mutual destruction of the planet, the crocodile slept.
