Triumph

by Cory Davidson
Joe spends many hours designing a highly classified fighter jet. The aerodynamics and arsenal capabilities designed are far superior to others. During one of many flights - Joe, climbing into the cockpit, notices a splat of white _stuff_ on the instrumentation. He cleans it off and then flys off - only to crash and burn.
