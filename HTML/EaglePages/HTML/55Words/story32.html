White Noise

by Darrin Mossor
The signals travelled through space till we received them from the little blue planet. Eventually we decoded them, making sense of the messages. At last, fellow intelligences!

Art, philosophy, wisdom. Anger, judgement, hate. All traveling at the same speed, in all directions.

Hundreds of light years and nothing on TV.
