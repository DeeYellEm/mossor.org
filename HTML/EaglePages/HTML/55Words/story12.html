Untitled #10 - Untitled #10

by Charlie Comstock
Kissing, hugging, groping, clutching, the lovers paused for breath. He broke from her arms, smiling.

"I have a surprise for you."

A moment later he placed a bridal catalog in her hands.

"I think we should get married."

"Oh honey...., I've been thinking that too, for a while now. But who would have us?"
