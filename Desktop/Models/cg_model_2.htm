A Dartboard and Darts

Here's a nice model of a bristle Dartboard and Darts with aluminum shafts.

The dartboard is pretty patch heavy at 6800 patches, but there is a silly amount of detail in the board itself. There is a color decal for the colors on the background that turned out pretty well (all from scratch). The darts are a bit lighter (at only 500 patches) and have a single bone per dart.

Some facts:

  • A decal for color and bump on the dartboard
  • Color material for the metal rim and others for the darts
  • Simple three light choreography

You should soon be able to find a copy of it for general use at http://www.AnimationPitstop.com
