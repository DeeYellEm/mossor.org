Southwest Sunrise

Date: 03/03/02

This is a pretty simple still. Just a single grecian-style urn on a marble stand in a museum somewhere.

The main goals were to play with lighting, trying to get softer shadows and to play with some semi-realistic decals for the urn.

It's a simple three light setup, with the main light having 9 rays and a width of 60 inches. The other two are smaller and only three rays each.
