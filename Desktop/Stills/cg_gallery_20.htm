Contest: Blue Rose

Date: 07/31/02

Below you'll find my entry for the A:M Image Contest for July of 2002. The theme was "Blue".

Blue Rose

Click on the image for a larger version (100k)

With this theme I wanted to come up with something that would not overwhelm, so the plan was to come up with a composition that would have just a touch of blue at the focal point. After playing around with sketches and other ideas, I came up with the idea of a blue rose (which doesn't exist in the real world) and created a still life around that. I tried to add just enough detail in the background and surrounding to balance the image and try to tell a story.

I'm pretty happy with the results.
