Creature Feature

This was a simple experiment that had the following goals:

  • Create a creature from scratch
  • Skin him in something approaching photo-realistic fashion

The results are not perfect. There are some flaws, specifically with some seams between decals that are more visible than I like (the left foreleg specifically).

Like most things in life, doing a good job skinning is far harder than it looks. Doing more than just a haphazard job is a very time consuming, detail-oriented task that requires a great deal of patience. These requirements leave me ill-suited for this, but I still have fun.
