Eyeballs!

Date: 10/28/01

This eye model has been submitted to the Animation Pitstop (www.AnimationPitstop.com).

Details:

  • Models separate cornea, iris, pupil and white of the eye
  • The iris has a slider to allow opening and closing the pupil
  • The iris uses the ToonNation Eyeball2 material
  • It uses a simple Environmental Map for simulated reflections on the eyeball
  • It has a single bone so you can apply a constraint to make it point at a null
  • It uses a transparent cornea
  • It also includes another set of eyes with lids, each of which has a separate slider to control how open/closed the lids are
  • It's pretty similar to what you might find in a Pixar eyeball - and it looks pretty good

As always, all feedback, positive and negative is appreciated and welcome here. Thanks, Darrin Mossor
