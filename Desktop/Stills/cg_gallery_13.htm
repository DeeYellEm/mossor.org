Rockem' Sockem'

Date: 10/03/01

This is my entry in the September 2001 A:M Monthly Image Contest. The theme is "Toys"

The robots are from scratch, trying to come up with my own design and some appropriately aged textures. The robots still need some work (especially the rigging, which was a bit rushed for the contest). The Rockem Sockem Robots ring is was modeled from a very small image I found on the net. The boxers were derived from Alain Desrochers' Shazam model.

Update [10/15/01]: This image took third in the A:M Image Contest. See the other entries here.
